## Accepted File Formats

* **3D Printing:** .STL (StereoLithography), .OBJ (Object)
* **Laser Cutting:** .SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics), .PDF (Portable Document Format)
* **CNC Milling:** .SVG
* **Electronic Cutter:** .SVG
